Understanding Digital Marketing Strategy

A digital marketing strategy is a data-driven plan that helps businesses achieve online growth. It encompasses:

  • •

    Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Enhancing website visibility on search engines.

  • •

    Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC): Driving traffic through paid ads.

  • •

    Content Marketing: Creating valuable content to attract and retain customers.

  • •

    Email Marketing: Nurturing leads and maintaining customer relationships.

  • •

    Analytics & Performance Tracking: Monitoring and optimizing campaign performance.
